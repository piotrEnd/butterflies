## butterflies
----

**Information architecture:**

Preserving butterflies is inarguably a niche and preparing a specimen is kind of arts and crafts. 
Main page presents steps in that process: catch, selection, preparation and exposition. It clearly demonstrates required effort to create an individual exemplar and furthermore justifies potential higher price. Short portrait of craftsman itself shortens mentally distance to consumer.
Next page presents features of the product and offers online shopping with paypal-checkout. Third page covers sideline activity – organizing exhibitions + contains section of testimonials. Last page holds contact data.

**Design:**

Multi-page design is well-suited for widening business allowing easy further scalability.
Pages have similar structure but distinct linear-gradients on each separate one. It refers to variety of species and – in a way - to their glitter colors.
